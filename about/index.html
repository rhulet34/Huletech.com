About Huletech

Who We Are

Huletech is a start-up tech company by Ryan Hulet. As a student, Ryan had the idea to create a more affordable computer repair option. Many places can charge ridiculous amounts for simple repairs, so why not offer the same service, with the same skill, for less--by cutting overhead costs.
