Hardware Repair

Hardware repair encompasses what ever needs done to an electronic to device to get it working properly. This includes laptops, desktops, mobile phones, gaming consoles, circuit boards, etc. Repair time may vary depending on the severity of the issue. At any given time under normal load, one could expect their device back within 5 business days. Everything needed for a repair or to progress in one is told to the customer before anything is started. This allows the customer to decide whether or not they think it is worth the cost to proceed.

This service is not limited to just fixing broken things. We also offer upgrades of all kinds (i.e. batteries, RAM, hard-drives). Call or stop by to discuss pricing on such jobs!

Mobile Repair

Mobile Repair is a little more limited than general hardware repair. At Huletech, on most phones, we can replace screens, batteries, speakers, charging ports, and cameras.

Software Installs

Software Installs takes the hassle of calibrating unfamiliar programs away from the user. We offer a streamlined installation process for a small fee. Installations can usually be done on the spot, at no additional charge. OS installs usually take longer, but can be done within 3 business days.

Virus Removal

Ever get a nasty virus that ruins your computing experience? No longer. With Huletech Virus Removal you can kiss your virus nightmares goodbye! Bring your machine by and we will have it back to normal within 5 business days.

Every virus removal job will also qualify for a free software optimization cycle. You will get your computer back running even smoother than before!

Data Backup

Our data backup service is only limited by transfer speeds. Whenever you being us a data backup job, we start the process immediately, and will return your backed-up data as soon as it completes!

Note: Client must bring their own device to have their data backed-up to, or they can special order a device through Huletech.
